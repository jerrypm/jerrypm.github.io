Strategic Tic-Tac-Toe with a Twist

Infinite XOX is a free online puzzle game based on classic Tic-Tac-Toe. Each player can only have 3 pieces on the board. When you place a 4th piece, your oldest one vanishes—adding deep strategy to every move!

Perfect for fans of logic games, brain teasers, and quick browser games. Play against a friend on the same device—no signup required.

Why Play Infinite XOX?

  • Strategic Depth: Unlike traditional Tic-Tac-Toe, every move matters as pieces disappear
  • Quick Games: Each round takes just minutes, perfect for short breaks
  • No Download: Play instantly in your browser on any device

Game Features

This strategic twist on the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game includes:

  • Smart AI opponent with multiple difficulty levels
  • Real-time piece tracking and visual indicators
  • Smooth animations and modern UI design
  • Score tracking across multiple rounds
  • Responsive design that works on mobile and desktop
